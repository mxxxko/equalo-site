Restore a Purchase

If you've already purchased Unlimited Mode and it isn't showing as unlocked, here's how to restore it:

  1. Open Equalo on your device
  2. Scroll to the Unlimited Mode section on the home screen
  3. Tap the unlock button
  4. On the paywall, tap "Restore Previous Purchase"

Make sure you're signed into the same Apple ID used for the original purchase.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I play?

Drag blocks from the bank onto the grid so every row and column forms a valid equation. Tap a block in the bank to rotate it. Tap a placed block on the grid to pick it up again.

What are the gold-bordered cells?

Those are anchor cells — they're fixed in place and can't be moved. Use them as reference points to figure out where the other blocks belong.

Why does my streak say 0?

Streaks require completing at least one puzzle per day. If you missed a day, the streak resets. Both daily and unlimited puzzle completions count toward your streak.

Does Equalo work offline?

Yes — no internet connection is required to play. Puzzles are generated on-device.

Is there a new daily puzzle every day?

Yes. A fresh puzzle is available at midnight local time for each difficulty level.
