Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 26, 2026
The short version: Equalo does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. Everything stays on your device.

1. Who We Are

Equalo is an iOS puzzle game developed and published as an independent application. For questions about this policy, contact us at equalosupport@gmail.com.

2. Data We Collect

Equalo does not collect any personal information. The following game data is stored locally on your device only using Apple's standard on-device storage (UserDefaults):

  • Puzzle completion history and solve times
  • Current and best streak counts
  • Selected difficulty preferences
  • In-progress puzzle state
  • Display preferences (e.g. dark mode setting)

This data never leaves your device and is never transmitted to any server.

3. Data We Do Not Collect

  • No name, email address, or contact information
  • No location data
  • No device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • No usage analytics or crash reporting
  • No third-party tracking or advertising SDKs

4. In-App Purchases

Equalo offers an optional one-time in-app purchase (Unlimited Mode) processed entirely through Apple's App Store and StoreKit framework. We do not see, store, or process your payment information. Apple's privacy policy governs all payment and purchase data.

You can review Apple's privacy policy at apple.com/legal/privacy.

5. Children's Privacy

Equalo does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children under the age of 13. Because no personal data is collected at all, the app is safe for all ages from a data privacy perspective.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

All game data is stored locally on your device. You can delete all associated data at any time by uninstalling the app from your device. We have no access to your data and therefore cannot delete it on your behalf.

7. Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, the revised version will be posted at this URL with an updated date. We encourage you to review it periodically. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

8. Your Rights

Because Equalo does not collect or store any personal data on external servers, there is no personal data for us to provide, correct, or delete upon request. If you have questions or concerns, please contact us at equalosupport@gmail.com.
